Clubs on Campus I am In

I am in way too many clubs and my time is often spread thin. This is what I think of some of them.

Here are the top 5 ranked

  1. CrossFit Club
  2. OASIS
  3. Adaptive Athletics Club
  4. Triathlon Club
  5. Chess Club

CrossFit

CrossFit was created in 2000 in Santa Cruz, California. It's founders, Greg Glassman and Lauren Jenai, combined gymnastics with weightlifting.

The CrossFit Club at Pitt was founded in 2008 and has been providing free fitness classes for all ability levels.

OASIS

OASIS stands for Official Association for Students in Information Science. It was founded in 2020 as a space for IS students.

They bring in tech professionals to give talks and network opportunities.

Adaptive Athletics

Adaptive Athletics was created in February 2025 as there did not exist a space for people in sports who are disabled.

They provide education to people about different para and adaptive sports as well as doing said para and adaptive sports.

Triathlon

Triathlons consist of running, swimming, and biking. It's all cardio.

Triathlon club provides students a way to participate in Triathlons at a cheaper rate along with group training times.

Chess Club

Chess is a complex game of moving and capturing pieces. It has existed for centuries.

Chess Club hosts Chess Tournaments and has open Chess games.
