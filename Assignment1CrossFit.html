CrossFit Club

CrossFit Club exemplifies the CrossFit methodology. Fitness should be high intensity, constantly variaried, and functional. CrossFit Club also make sure it is accessible to the overall community.

I love CrossFit club. It educated me in personal fitness as well as gave a me a community, but I also think sometimes we do stupid and dangerous stuff.
